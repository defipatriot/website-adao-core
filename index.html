Install as App

Add Alliance DAO to your home screen for fullscreen, app-like access — no browser bar, your own icon, native feel.

iPhone / iPad

  1. Open thealliancedao.com in Safari (must be Safari, not Chrome)
  2. Tap the Share button at the bottom
  3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
  4. Tap Add in the top right

Android

  1. Open thealliancedao.com in Chrome
  2. Tap the menu in the top right
  3. Tap "Install app" (or "Add to Home Screen")
  4. Tap Install to confirm

Tip: A "Install app" banner often appears automatically — you can just tap that.

Quick Launch Shortcuts (Android only)

Once installed, long-press the app icon on your home screen to get instant shortcuts to:

  • NFT Explorer
  • TLA Stats
  • DAO Governance

iOS doesn't support long-press shortcuts yet, but you can navigate normally inside the installed app.
